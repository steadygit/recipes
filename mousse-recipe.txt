


		Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients:
  4 eggs
  6 oz. chocolate (170 gm)
  1 oz. butter
  Juice of 1 orange
  1 tsp ground coffee
  Grated orange peel
  6 small mousse dishes

Directions:
  Melt the chocolate in a fireproof disk in a low oven.
  When soft, stir in the coffee and grated orange peel.
  Stir in the 4 yolks, then stir in the butter.  Next stir
  in the orange juice.

  Beat four egg whites and fold them in.  Pour the mixture
  into little dishes and chill until you serve.
